01 / About

Profile Overview

Dr. Adel Al-Hezmi received his PhD in 2011 and MSc in electrical engineering in 2003 from the Technische Universität Berlin, Germany, with specific emphasis on telecommunication platforms and networks. He has more than twenty years of working experience as a system, solution, and enterprise architect.

He has worked within the framework of large-scale projects with international players in different countries by contributing to several architecture domains including business, information, application, technical, integration, security and software architecture. Solutions follow top-down, bottom-up or mixed approaches, always aligned with the client organization strategy across different value streams.

The architecture embodies specific agile principles, relies on state-of-the-art technologies, and focuses on customer satisfaction and built-in quality and compliance. In most project activities, Adel has been involved with the client in all project phases ranging from project proposals and pre-sales to RFI/RFP responses and project delivery.

Currently working at Qatar Mobility Innovations Center (QMIC) as a Senior Solution Architect and Technology Advisor since May 2022.
